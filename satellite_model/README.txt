satellite_model

———————————————————————————————————————

An clear animated model showing movement and physics of a satellite orbiting Mars.
The velocities, energies and positions in both moving and inertial frames are calculated.

The initial conditions can be changed by modifying the value of some variables in the code.
The mass of the satellite and the planet can also be changed such that other planetary/galactic orbit can be simulated.

———————————————————————————————————————

Required Python libraries:

- NumPy
- SciPy
- matplotlib
