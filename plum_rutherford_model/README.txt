plum_rutherford_model

———————————————————————————————————————

Simulates the gold leaf experiment with either plum pudding or rutherford's atomic model.
Alpha particles are deflected by an array of gold atoms. The electric field from each gold atom are included in the motion of the alpha particles, hence it will take long to run.

———————————————————————————————————————

Required Python libraries:

- NumPy
- SciPy
- matplotlib
